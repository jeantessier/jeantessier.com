Martin Fowler

The author of:

  • Refactoring: Improving the Design of Existing Code
  • Analysis Patterns
  • UML Distilled

and more recently of:

  • Patterns of Enterprise Application Architecture

A very cool guy with a no-nonsense approach to software development.

His home page is at http://www.martinfowler.com/.
