Jean Tessier @ Gusto

I have been working at Gusto in San Francisco, CA, since October 2019.

Gusto's San Francisco office is on historic Pier 70, in the old machine shop of Bethlehem Shipbuilding's Union Iron Works shipyard. We get to enjoy a giant gantry crane, rated for 50 tons, suspended above our heads every day. The Historical Society won't let us play with it, though. :-(
